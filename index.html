Funteron is a digital entertainment group built for the new era of sports engagement. The name combines “fun” with teron — the Esperanto word for “earth” — reflecting our mission to deliver globally accessible, intelligent, and rewarding experiences for sports fans everywhere.

Funteron brings together advanced technology, gamified experiences, and affiliate monetization to unlock new revenue for media companies, telcos, and betting operators. Our first products, OddsComparisonHub (OCH) and The Bracket Hub (TBH), offer white-labeled, scalable platforms that convert passive audiences into active, monetizable communities.
